Fried Rice

Make the most umami bomb meal with just 5 simple ingredients. And to top it off, make this in just under 5 minutes of prep and 5 minutes of cook. Here are the list of ingredients

  • Egg
  • Day old white rice
  • Msg
  • Green oinions
  • Soy Sauce
Very simple and we will start cooking so follow along!

  1. Heat up oil in a pan, cook the egg white and egg yolk seperately
  2. Take eggs out and begin cooking the rice and keep tossing it within the wok/pan
  3. Add back the cooked eggs and season with a pinch of msg and two table spoons of soysauce
Now enjoy!
