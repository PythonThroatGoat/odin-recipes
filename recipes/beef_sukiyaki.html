Beef Sukiyaki

This beauty here is called beef sukiyaki. It is mainly made with a soy sauce based broth in which the high grade thinly sliced beef will be cooked to a perfect medium rare. It is sweet due to the combination of marin and sugar with a hint of sake that makes this an irresistible dish. Other highlights include the flexibility of the other ingredients in the pot. It is a one pot meal, where you can have noodles, mushrooms, tofu and even other protiens to make it an even heartier meal.

My recipe will gurantee you the fastest and easiest way to make Beef Sukiyaki here are some of the neccessary ingredients you need.

  • 2 tablespoon Sake
  • 1/2 cup of Mirin
  • 1/2 cup of Soy sauce
  • 2 table spoon of Brown sugar
  • Crap ton of High Grade slice rib eye
  • Assortment of preferred sides

You can make this one pot meal in these 6 simple steps

  1. In a pan bring your sake, mirin and soy sauce to a light boil
  2. Turn off the heat and transfer the sexy sauce to a bowl
  3. Prepare all your sides like your mushrooms, noodles and tofu
  4. Soak your noodles in the water for 10 minutes
  5. In the same pan add oil and lightly sear your thin beef for about 10 seconds
  6. Toss in that beautiful sauce and your sides. Then get ready for a feast!
